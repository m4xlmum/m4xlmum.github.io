Empress is known for cracking such games as Mortal Kombat 11 and Total War: Three Kingdoms. Her biggest breakthrough was with Red Dead Redemption 2, which she hacked a year after release when many tried to do the same before her but didn’t succeed.

Empress以破解《真人快打 11》和《全面战争：三国》等游戏而闻名。她最大的突破是《荒野大镖客：救赎2》，这部游戏在发售一年后被她黑了，当时在她之前有很多人都尝试过，但都以失败告终。

Hogwarts Legacy seems doomed to be released by pirates in just a few days. A known hacker Empress announced beta-testing of her version entering its second phase, meaning the Harry Potter game is already cracked.

《霍格沃茨之遗》似乎注定要在短短几天内被盗版。一位著名的黑客Empress宣布她的版本进入第二阶段测试，这意味着这款哈利·波特游戏已经破解。

此前，Empress警告“华纳 碧池”，她将在短短10天内拿下新的Denuvo防御。如果真如她所说，那么她已经提前完成了。很可能一旦测试完成，她就会授权给每个人玩这个游戏。

Hogwarts Legacy is not the first victim of Empress. Previously, she did it with titles such as Mortal Kombat 11, Total War: Three Kingdoms, and Red Dead Redemption 2. She also claims to speed up future cracks because she “enters an entirely new phase,” targeting the major upcoming titles. “I’m putting my personal projects aside,” and “I will not mess around with 6 months or 1 year later cracks,” says Empress’ statement.
